## User Types

  <details>
     <summary>Creator Profile</summary>
      <blockquote>
        <details>
          <summary>Why would I need a creator profile?</summary>
          <blockquote>
          Signing up for a creator profile allows you to offer your resources on the platform.
          </blockquote>
        </details>

        <details>
          <summary>How do I create a creator profile?</summary>
          <blockquote>
          Click the menu button in the upper right corner and click *Create Creator
            Profile* and provide the requested information.
          </blockquote>
        </details>

        <details>
          <summary>Who can see my creator information?</summary>
          <blockquote>
          All registered users can visit your creator profile which shows your creator profile information such as image, listed resources, and about.
          
          </blockquote>
        </details>

        <details>
          <summary>How do I change my creator profile?</summary>
          <blockquote>
          Click the menu button in the upper right corner and click *My Creator
            Profile*, on the profile page click the pencil icon, change the necessary fields then click the save icon.

            <strong>Please note edits will not be saved if you do not click the</strong><em> Save Profile</em><strong> button!</strong>
          </blockquote>
        </details>
      </blockquote>
    </details>
